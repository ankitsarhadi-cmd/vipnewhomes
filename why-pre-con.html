Why Buy Pre-Construction?

15 reasons buyers and investors consistently choose new over resale — starting with the one that changes everything.

#1 Reason

Government HST Rebate on New Construction Homes

The federal government's New Housing HST Rebate allows eligible buyers to recover a significant portion of the HST paid on a newly built home. For primary residence purchases, this rebate can be worth up to $130,000 — a cost advantage that simply doesn't exist when buying resale. This single benefit dramatically shifts the affordability calculation for many first-time buyers and investors.

$130K
Max Rebate

Financial Advantages

📈

Lock In Today's Price

You sign at today's price. If the market appreciates during the 12–36 months of construction, you benefit from that growth before paying the balance at closing. Many investors have arrived at closing with significant equity — without ever touching the property.

Appreciation Play
🗓️

Staggered Deposits — Not a Lump Sum

Pre-construction deposits (typically 15–20%) are spread over 12–24 months, not due all at once. This gives you more time to save, arrange financing, and manage cash flow compared to a large immediate down payment on a resale purchase.

Cash Flow Friendly
🤝

No Bidding Wars

Pre-construction eliminates multiple-offer stress entirely. You negotiate directly with the builder at published pricing — no emotional overpaying, no losing to a cash buyer, no sleepless nights. The price is the price.

Stress-Free Buying
🎁

Builder Incentives Not Available in Resale

Builders regularly offer packages to move inventory — free upgrades, appliance packages, capped development charges, extended deposit structures, and assignment clauses. These incentives can be worth tens of thousands of dollars and are negotiated through your agent at no cost to you.

Added Value

The Property Itself

🛡️

Full Tarion Warranty Coverage

Every new Ontario home comes with mandatory Tarion protection: 1 year on workmanship and materials, 2 years on systems (electrical, plumbing, HVAC), and 7 years on major structural defects. No equivalent protection exists when buying resale.

Ontario Law
🔧

No Surprise Repairs in Early Years

With a new home, there are no hidden roof, HVAC, plumbing, or foundation surprises. Everything is built to current code, under warranty, and inspected. The unpredictable repair costs that define older resale homes simply don't apply.

Lower Risk
⚡

Modern Finishes, Energy Efficiency & Smart Tech

New builds meet modern energy codes — lower utility bills, stronger insulation, energy-efficient appliances, and smart home infrastructure that would cost significantly more to retrofit into an older property. Better for tenants, better for resale.

Built to Code 2024+
🎨

Customize Before It's Built

Choose your finishes, flooring, cabinetry, and upgrades at a builder decor appointment — before construction begins. You get a home tailored to your preferences, not someone else's renovation choices from 15 years ago.

Your Way
