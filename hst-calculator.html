Your Purchase Details

New Ontario rules (Apr 1, 2026–Mar 31, 2027): Full 13% HST rebate on homes up to $1M. Flat $130K rebate from $1M–$1.5M. Declining rebate from $1.5M to $24K at $1.85M+. Applies to primary residence and residential rental.
