How Pre-Construction Actually Works

Six steps from registration to keys — with our team beside you the entire way.

1

Register for VIP Access — Free

Fill out a quick registration with your budget, preferred city, and home type. There's no cost, no obligation, and no pressure. You're added to our VIP list and matched against active and upcoming projects before they open to the public.

✓ Takes less than 2 minutes. No commitment required.
2

Get Matched Before the Public Sees It

When a project matches your profile, we reach out before it goes public. You'll get first access to floor plans, lot maps, pricing, and incentives — often 24 to 72 hours ahead of general release. The best lots, models, and elevations always go first. VIP Access is the difference between choosing your home and settling for what's left.

✓ 27 active projects across Peel, Halton, York Region and beyond.
3

Review Everything Together

Before you sign anything, we walk you through every detail — builder track record, community location, floor plan value, upgrade options, deposit structure, and fine print. Our advice is honest: if a project doesn't suit your situation, we'll tell you. Our job is service, not commission.

✓ 5.0 stars across 164 Google reviews — service over sales, always.
4

Sign at the Sales Office — With Ankit Present

We attend every signing with you. You'll review the Agreement of Purchase and Sale (APS) together — a legal contract between you and the builder. We'll also connect you with a trusted real estate lawyer from our network to review the agreement and protect your interests before you finalize.

✓ A real estate lawyer reviews your APS before you're bound to anything.
5

Pay Deposits Over Time — While It's Being Built

Unlike resale, pre-construction deposits are spread over months — not due all at once. A typical Ontario builder deposit structure looks like this:

Milestone Amount (example on $900K) Timing
On Signing $25,000 – $45,000 Day of signing
2nd Deposit $25,000 – $45,000 90 days
3rd Deposit $25,000 – $45,000 180 days
4th Deposit (if applicable) Balance to 20% 365 days
✓ Deposit structures vary by builder. We review yours before you sign.
6

Close, Move In, and Enjoy Your New Home

On closing day, your lawyer handles the final paperwork and transfer of title. You pay the remaining balance (typically financed through your mortgage), receive your keys, and move into a brand-new home covered by Ontario's Tarion warranty — 1 year on workmanship, 2 years on systems, and 7 years on major structure.

✓ Tarion warranty protection on every new Ontario home.
