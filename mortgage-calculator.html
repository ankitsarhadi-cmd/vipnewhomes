HST Rebate Calculator

New Ontario rules in effect April 1, 2026 – March 31, 2027. Full 13% rebate on homes up to $1M — max $130,000.

Purchase Details

New Ontario rules (Apr 1, 2026 – Mar 31, 2027): Full 13% HST rebate on homes up to $1M. Flat $130K rebate from $1M–$1.5M. Declining rebate from $1.5M to $24K at $1.85M+.
